Senior Frontend Developer

TechNova Solutions — Lagos, Nigeria

Full Time ₦350k - ₦500k Remote Available

Job Description

TechNova Solutions is seeking a highly skilled and creative Senior Frontend Developer to join our fast-growing engineering team. You will work with modern technologies to build high-quality interfaces for global clients.

Responsibilities

  • Build responsive and scalable web interfaces.
  • Collaborate with backend engineers and designers.
  • Optimize applications for speed and performance.
  • Maintain and improve company website and dashboards.
  • Implement UI/UX designs with pixel-perfect precision.

Required Skills

  • Expert in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Tailwind, JavaScript.
  • Strong experience with React or Vue.js.
  • Understanding of REST APIs and Git workflow.
  • Knowledge of modern UI/UX principles.
  • Strong problem-solving and communication skills.

Qualifications

  • 3+ years of experience in frontend development.
  • Portfolio of past frontend projects.
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science or related field (optional).
